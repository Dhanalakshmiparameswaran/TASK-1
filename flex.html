Lavender Flowers

Lavender flowers are bright violet in colour, and have a fresh, sweet fragrance.
It has a calming effect on the senses.Because of this quality, lavender extract
and scent are frequently used in making talcum powders, perfumes,
essential bath oils, spa treatments, air freshener, etc.
The fragrance of lavender is said to relax the mind and ease stress.
